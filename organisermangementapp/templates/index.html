Recent Causes

Here are some causes or projects we've embarked on.


Hairdressing & Barbing Training

Intensive training by MDF prodigious trainees on hand-on skills like hairdressing, barbing, computer training, carpentry and other entrepreneurship skills.

DRILLING OF BOREHOLES

Construction of borehole and water pumps for deprived communities. The boreholes is estimated at $ 3,845 each.

Donations

Donation of stationaries to those interested in becoming hairdressers and barbers. Items required to start up little businesses are provided.

Agricultural Training

This is part of the objectives of MDF, it includes the provision several agricultural equipments, skills, training in crop production, fish and livestock farming. Participants will have seamless access to loan after undergoing training.
