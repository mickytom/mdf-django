who we are?

MANNERS DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION offers the following:

Daily Arabic and Quranic E-Learning Program (DAQELP)

DAQELP is a daily Arabic and Quranic program that teaches how to read and write Arabic Language and perfect recitation of the Holy Quran within a short period of time, even without prior knowledge. The program is well organized to facilitate learning process for underprivileged individuals who are either novice or slow Arabic language readers. In addition, Arabic alphabets and signs, such as Fatha, Kasroh, Dommah, Sukun, Fathatan, Kasrotan, Dommatan, Sukun, Alif Modd, Wao Modd, Yao Modd, and Shaddah are also parts of our priorities. Therefore, we strongly believe that this program will strengthen the participants’ knowledge in reading and understanding Arabic and Qur’an. The MDF is working in collaboration with some Arab countries to bring in competent and professional foreign teachers to attain the Mission and Vision of the MDF.

About CEO

Sheik Muyideen Alhashimi

Born on the 23rd of January an apostle of change, a revolutionary of our time and a highly focused personality in administering people. He is a quintessence of humility, an apostle of change, a revolutionary of our time and a highly focused personality in administering people. Exceptional in diligently reshaping today for a brighter tomorrow while taking clue from the past. An ardent believer in training his subordinates and assigning them responsibilities through effective delegation for the creation of an incorruptible, efficient, resilient and responsive service system.

The name is as simple as the bearer himself. He is known, called and addressed as Alhaji Muyideen. Born in Saki, Oyo state on 23rd of January 1959, he completed his early education at the Idara Primary School, Idara in Bukina Faso. He proceeded to the then Islamic University of Imam Muhammad Ibn Saud Secondary School, now Saudi Islamic Secondary School, Riyadh, Saudi Arabic where his immense talents in Arabian language and culture became apparent within a very short spell of time. He bagged his Bachelor of Art (BA) Degree in Mass Communication from the prestigious United Arab Emirates University, Abu Dhabi, UAE in 1987 and a Master of Art (MA) in Arabic Language and Culture from Lagos State University in 2005.

MUYIDEEN A. ASHIMI has also worked with home and away organization and currently Chairman and Founder of Manners Development Foundation (http://mannersfoundation.org) (NGO), well registered in which is partnering with United Arab Emirate to support the Internal Displaced Person (IDP) with some gadget and mobile Ambulance in Nigeria.
