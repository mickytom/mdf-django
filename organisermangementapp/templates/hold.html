Daily Arabic and Quranic E-Learning Program (DAQELP)

DAQELP is a daily Arabic and Quranic program that teaches how to read and write Arabic Language and perfect recitation of the Holy Quran within a short period of time, even without prior knowledge. The program is well organized to facilitate learning process for underprivileged individuals who are either novice or slow Arabic language readers. In addition, Arabic alphabets and signs, such as Fatha, Kasroh, Dommah, Sukun, Fathatan, Kasrotan, Dommatan, Sukun, Alif Modd, Wao Modd, Yao Modd, and Shaddah are also parts of our priorities. Therefore, we strongly believe that this program will strengthen the participants’ knowledge in reading and understanding Arabic and Qur’an. The MDF is working in collaboration with some Arab countries to bring in competent and professional foreign teachers to attain the Mission and Vision of the MDF.
